Projects

7 Wonders score sheet

7 Wonders is a board game where each player is the leader of a great city in the ancient world. You build up seven different aspects of your city (science, commerce, military, etc.) trying to get the most points by the end of the game.

I made a score sheet using React and Redux to add up your points without wasting paper.

Burgle Bros Recon

Burgle Bros is a cooperative board game, where a team of thieves explore an office building to rob safes. The game provides a reference card of how many of each room tile is in the game, so you can look at what's been explored and guess what the next tile can be. But counting all the explored tiles is tedious. Similarly, you can look at where the guard has been to guess where they'll go next but combing through a discard pile each time is not fun.

I built a digital reference using React and Redux to keep track of rooms and guard destinations. Click on rooms as you explore to keep track of what you've revealed, and see at a glance what's left. For the guard destinations, click on tiles as they come up in the guard deck and see the remaining possibilities.

Phonetic Speller

My full last name is a mess (ask me about it). When I call customer support for anything I want to use the phonetic alphabet to spell it out precisely, but I can never remember the code words.

I created a tool where you enter a word or phrase and get the code words for each of its letters. No more ambiguity when pulling up my account.
