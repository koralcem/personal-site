Pizza Area Value

Should you spend a bit more and get the bigger size pizza? Is it worth it?

Neil deGrasse Tyson has this great quote about the issue. The math obviously checks out, but calculating the area of a circle quickly isn't easy for a lot of people. The Pizza Area Value app lets you input the diameters and prices of two pizza sizes you're considering and get a comparison instantly.
